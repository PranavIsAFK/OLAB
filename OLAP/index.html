Technical Insights

The Knapsack Challenge

In logistics, the 0/1 Knapsack Problem is fundamental. We must choose which packages to load into a vehicle with limited capacity to maximize profit. "0/1" means a package is either fully taken or left behind—it cannot be divided.

Why Dynamic Programming?

Unlike greedy approaches that can be fooled by high-value items that take up too much space, DP explores all possibilities efficiently by reusing solutions to smaller sub-problems, guaranteeing the mathematical optimum.
